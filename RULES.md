# Rang Adda - Official Rules

## 1. Bluff (Cheat / I Doubt It / BS)

### Objective
Be the first player to get rid of all your cards by either telling the truth or successfully bluffing about the cards you play.

### Players & Deck
*   **Players:** 2–6 players (Modified from 3-8 per project specifications).
*   **Deck:** 1 standard 52-card deck.

### Setup
*   Shuffle the deck.
*   Deal all cards as evenly as possible.
*   Players may arrange their cards in any order.
*   Choose a starting player randomly or let the player with the Ace of Spades start.

### Turn Order
*   Play proceeds clockwise.
*   The rank being played follows this cycle:
    `Ace → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5 → 6 → 7 → 8 → 9 → 10 → Jack → Queen → King → Ace → ...`

### How a Turn Works
On your turn:
1.  Place 1 to 4 cards face down into the center pile.
2.  Announce what you are playing (e.g., "Two 5s").
    *   You may tell the truth, lie completely, or play some correct and some incorrect cards.
    *   No one can look at the cards unless a bluff is called.

### Calling Bluff
Before the next player starts their turn, any player may call "Bluff!" or "I doubt it!".
The last played cards are revealed.
*   **If the player lied:** They pick up the entire center pile.
*   **If the player told the truth:** The player who called Bluff picks up the entire center pile.
*   Afterward, play continues with the next player in normal order.

### Passing
If you genuinely have no reasonable play or if your group allows it, you may pass.
*   **House Rule:** If every player passes consecutively, discard the center pile without revealing it. Start a new pile beginning with the next required rank.

### Winning
A player wins immediately when:
1.  They have no cards left.
2.  Their final play is not successfully challenged.
If someone calls Bluff on the final play:
*   If truthful, that player wins.
*   If a lie, they pick up the pile and the game continues.
